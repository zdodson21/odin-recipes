Description

Air frier fries that I love to eat

Ingredients

  • Fries
  • Olive Oil Spray
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Poweder
  • Season Salt
  • Steak Seasoning

Steps

  1. Place fries into air fryer tray
  2. Spray with olive oil, the mix with hands
  3. Mix in paprika, garlic powder, season salt, and steak seasoning. All to taste, and stir after adding each spice
