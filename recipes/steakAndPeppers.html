Steak & Peppers Recipe

Go Back

Description

One of my favorite meals in college, I would go down to the local Target and pick up two bell peppers, and a pack of six small steaks. I would prepare this meal for my brother and myself, twice a week.

Ingredients

  • Steak of your choosing
  • 1/2 bell pepper per person
  • Steak seasoning
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Place butter in hot pan to melt
  2. Season steak with steak seasoning
  3. Place steak into pan
  4. Cut bell pepper into thin strips, lightly sprinkle on some steak seasoning
  5. After flipping steak, place cut bell pepper into pan with steak
  6. Cook until steak is at your preferred temperature
  7. Serve
