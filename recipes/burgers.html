Burger Recipe

Go Back

Description

This burger recipe is what I made every week in college. Burgers are one of my favorite foods, and I was always trying to make them better.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • Burger buns
  • Hamburger patty
  • Garlic Powder
  • Steak Seasoning
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickle slices
  • Burger sauce
    • Relish
    • Bacon bits
    • Mayonaise
    • Ketchup
    • Mustard

Steps

  1. Spray non-stick cooking spray onto heated pan
  2. Spread garlic powder on one side of burger, then steak seasoning on other side of the burger
  3. Place burger in pan, and flip when needed
  4. Mix small amount of relish, bacon bits, mayonaise, ketchup, & mustard together in small bowl
  5. Place burger buns in pan until toasted
  6. Place burger buns on plate, and place all ingredients together in whatever order you prefer in between buns
